# Caption
Romantic Hindi/Bollywood love ballad for a rainy-night city vibe. Warm acoustic guitar and gentle piano arpeggios lead, supported by soft tabla/dholak, mellow bass, airy pads, and lush string swells. Intimate vocals, but keep the instrumental bed fuller and a bit louder so the music feels present (bigger chorus lift, wide stereo, smooth reverb).

A romantic Indian pop ballad built on a foundation of gentle acoustic guitar arpeggios and a soft, steady drum machine beat. A smooth, emotive male lead vocal delivers a heartfelt melody, enhanced by subtle reverb and layered harmonies in the soaring chorus. The arrangement gradually introduces a warm bassline and atmospheric synth pads, creating a lush soundscape. A contrasting bridge section features a filtered, telephone-like vocal effect over a more sparse instrumentation before the song builds back to a powerful final chorus and fades out with a delicate acoustic guitar and flute-like synth melody.

An intimate and heartfelt acoustic folk-pop track featuring a clean, fingerpicked acoustic guitar that provides both the harmonic and rhythmic foundation. A clear, emotive male vocal delivers a melodic and sincere performance in Hindi. The arrangement is sparse and focused, with a brief, melodic acoustic guitar interlude midway through. The song maintains a gentle, introspective mood throughout, concluding with a decelerating, arpeggiated guitar figure that fades to silence.
